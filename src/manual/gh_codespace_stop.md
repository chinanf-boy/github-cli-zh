## gh codespace stop

Stop a running codespace

```
gh codespace stop [flags]
```

### Options


<dl class="flags">
	<dt><code>-c</code>, <code>--codespace &lt;string&gt;</code></dt>
	<dd>Name of the codespace</dd>
</dl>
