

## gh codespace logs

Access codespace logs

```
gh codespace logs [flags]
```

### Options


<dl class="flags">
	<dt><code>-c</code>, <code>--codespace &lt;string&gt;</code></dt>
	<dd>Name of the codespace</dd>

	<dt><code>-f</code>, <code>--follow</code></dt>
	<dd>Tail and follow the logs</dd>
</dl>
